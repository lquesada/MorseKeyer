# Privacy Policy for MorseKeyer

The **MorseKeyer** app (the "App") is provided by **Luis Quesada Torres** ("the Author") as an Open Source application. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

### 1. No Data Collection
The App is designed to be privacy-respecting. 
*   **Personal Data:** The Author does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, address, or location).
*   **Device Data:** The App does not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or technical telemetry.
*   **Offline Operation:** The App operates entirely locally on your device and does not require an internet connection to perform its core functions.

### 2. Third-Party Services
The App does **not** use any third-party service providers (such as advertising networks, analytics, or crash reporting SDKs) that could track your activity or collect data about you.

### 3. Permissions
Any permissions requested by the App on your Android device are used strictly for the local functionality of the Morse keying features. No data accessed via these permissions is ever uploaded, shared, or stored off the device.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
The App does not address anyone under the age of 13, and it does not knowingly collect any information from children. Because the App collects no data of any kind, it is compliant with global privacy standards regarding minors.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
The Author may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document and pushing the changes to the GitHub repository.

### 6. Contact
If you have questions regarding this policy, please open an **Issue** on the official GitHub repository link provided above.
